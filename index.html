About us


Sportzilla is a sports avenue management company owned by Samyak Innovations Pvt. Ltd & run by a team of young enthusiast sports lovers. We are a startup that builds & operates football and cricket turfs. We aim to create play-spaces of international standards, where citizens of our nation can practice, perform & develop their skills to bring glory to our nation and thereby contributing in creating a fit & healthy India.
